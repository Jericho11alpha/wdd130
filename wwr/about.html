At BoobyGee Rafting, we pride ourselves on providing top-notch equipment, personalized service, and a commitment to environmental stewardship. Whether you're a seasoned rafter or a first-timer, our diverse range of trips caters to all skill levels. Join us for an adventure of a lifetime and create memories that will last forever!

History

Founded in 2010, White Water Rafting began with a passion for adventure and the outdoors. Our founders wanted to share the thrill of river rafting with people from all walks of life.

Over the years, we have grown into a trusted company known for safety, professionalism, and unforgettable experiences. Our mission continues to bring excitement and connection through nature.
