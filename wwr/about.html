BoobyGee Rafting

Welcome to BoobyGee Rafting, your premier destination for exhilarating white water rafting adventures! Nestled in the heart of nature's most breathtaking rivers, we offer unforgettable experiences for thrill-seekers and outdoor enthusiasts alike. Our expert guides are passionate about sharing the excitement of navigating rapids while ensuring your safety and enjoyment.

At BoobyGee Rafting, we pride ourselves on providing top-notch equipment, personalized service, and a commitment to environmental stewardship. Whether you're a seasoned rafter or a first-timer, our diverse range of trips caters to all skill levels. Join us for an adventure of a lifetime and create memories that will last forever!

History

Founded in 2010 by a group of river enthusiasts, White Water Rafting started as a small local adventure company with just a few rafts. Over the years, we have grown into a trusted rafting provider, hosting hundreds of thrill-seekers each season. Our team combines passion, experience, and a love for the outdoors to make every trip safe, exciting, and unforgettable. From humble beginnings to becoming a premier rafting destination, our journey is driven by adventure, community, and the thrill of the river.
